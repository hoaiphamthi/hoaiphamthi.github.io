The nice voice

    The days of online teaching make communication with students mainly through voice. I think it was an interesting situation. We seem to be concentrate on just one sense - hearing instead of looking arround students as in classes. I realize that many students have beautiful voices, and imagine he/she must have beautiful soul matching that voice!
    How important the voice is! I remember that as I read the book "My Brief History" by Stephen Hawking, he said his thoughts when his muscular dystrophy made him increasingly difficult for speaking: "The voice of someone is very important. If you have a soft voice, others may see you as mentally unstable." This statement, of course, concerning the physical properties of the voice and is obviously suitable for a person with normal health.
    However, the beautiful voice that I refer here not only mentioning about the sound quality of the voice but also including the involved content - beautiful and honest words that make listeners feel very loved and comfortable....
    So between a nice voice and an eye-catching appearance, which one do you like?

    Hanoi, the afternoon of December 16, 2021
